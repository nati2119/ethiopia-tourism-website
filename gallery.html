Photography Tips for Ethiopia

Capture the best moments of your Ethiopian adventure with these photography tips.

Best Lighting

The golden hours (sunrise and sunset) provide the best light for photography in Ethiopia. The soft light enhances landscapes and portraits.

People Photography

Always ask for permission before photographing people. Ethiopians are generally friendly but appreciate being asked first.

Equipment

Bring a zoom lens for wildlife, a wide-angle for landscapes, and extra memory cards and batteries as electricity can be unreliable.

Photography Etiquette

Some religious sites restrict photography, especially inside churches. Always check with your guide or local authorities. Military installations, airports, and bridges should never be photographed.
